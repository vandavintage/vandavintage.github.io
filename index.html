Vanessa & Anne

We are close friends who together share a passion for the addictive world of vintage costume jewellery- timeless and often unique pieces which reflect the glamour and beauty of a bygone era. Between us we have over 30 years experience in luxury brand retail, and so decided to pool our knowledge and enthusiasm together to create V&A Vintage Costume Jewellery. We only source pieces which are genuinely signed with the maker s mark , thus giving our customers the chance to own not just a piece of beautiful jewellery, but also a little piece of history.

  • What we do...

    Our designers include Trifari, Weiss, Lisner, Coro, Kramer, Pierre Bex, Sphinx and many more.

    If you are looking for that glamorous touch of vintage designer sparkle, for your jewellery box, or to complete your look for a red carpet occasion or wedding day.
