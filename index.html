Vanessa & Anne

We are close friends who together share a passion for the addictive world of vintage costume jewellery- timeless and often unique pieces which reflect the glamour and beauty of a bygone era. Between us we have over 30 years experience in luxury brand retail, and so decided to pool our knowledge and enthusiasm together to create V&A Vintage Costume Jewellery. We only source pieces which are genuinely signed with the maker s mark , thus giving our customers the chance to own not just a piece of beautiful jewellery, but also a little piece of history.

Jewellery Designers

Our designers include Trifari, Weiss, Lisner, Coro, Kramer, Pierre Bex, Sphinx and many more.

If you are looking for that glamorous touch of vintage designer sparkle, for your jewellery box, or to complete your look for a red carpet occasion or wedding day.

Trifari

Founder, Gustavo Trifari in 1940. Head Designer Alfred Philippe previously worked at Cartier

Sherman

Founded by Gustave Sherman in 1947 in Canada, he favoured bold and extravagent designs

Selro

Founded in 1949 in the US by Paul Selenger, loved by many Hollywood film stars

Weiss

Founder, Albert Weiss in 1942. Detailed pieces featuring clear and coloured rhinestone

Hobe

Sought after for its fine quality worn by many 1950s movie stars

Coro

Founded in New York in the early 1900s, one of the 1920s largest costume jewellery companies

Sarah Coventry

Founder, Charles Stuart in 1949. Famously known as being "for the woman who dares to be different"

Lisner

Founded in New York in the early 1900s. Coverted for its individual Designes

Schoffel & Co

Founded in the 1930s and operated until the 1960s. Frequently represented as one of the reasons to do business with Austria
