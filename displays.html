The Bulkeley Hotel Beaumaris

V&A Vintage are very proud to exhibit our stunning collection of vintage costume jewellery in the display cabinets of the beautiful Bulkeley Hotel in the heart of Anglesey.

The Bulkeley Hotel is a grade 1 listed building and a fine example of Georgian elegance and old world charm of yesteryear

All jewellery can be purchased directly from the hotel itself but we also regularly attend the very popular Antiques and Collectors fairs which they hold each month. Please come and visit us where we can show new and exciting additions to our collection.
